Tacos

A taco is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla, typically made of corn or flour, folded or rolled and filled with various ingredients.

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon soy-based liquid seasoning (such as Maggi® Jugo)
  • ½ teaspoon chipotle powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Pineapple Salsa

  • 1 cup diced fresh pineapple
  • ¼ cup minced red onion
  • 1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • salt to taste
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. step 1Combine ground pork, liquid seasoning, ground chipotle powder, and garlic in a bowl. Set aside for flavors to meld.
  2. step 2Combine pineapple, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt in a bowl. Set pineapple salsa aside.
  3. step 3Preheat a large skillet over high heat. Crumble ground pork into the hot skillet. Cook and stir until pork is completely browned and edges begin to crisp, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess grease. Place pork in a bowl and keep warm. Wipe out skillet.
  4. step 4Warm tortillas in skillet over medium-low heat, about 20 seconds per side. Divide meat mixture between tortillas and top with pineapple salsa
